## [1.1.1] — 2026-06-24

### Fixed
- My Notes: fixed an issue where note rows stopped responding after returning from a video, so you can now open any note right away.

## [1.1.0] — 2026-06-23

### Added
- New My Notes screen: browse all your notes grouped by video, jump straight to any note's moment, and delete notes with a quick confirmation.
- Search your notes by title or text, with on-screen keyboard and voice dictation.
- Notes that include an image are now marked with an Image badge.
- Jumping to a note keeps your place — the video plays from the note while a Resume button lets you return to where you left off.

### Fixed
- Smoother playback start: videos resume at the right spot without flashing at the beginning.
- On-screen player controls no longer disappear when pressing past the top or bottom of the controls.
